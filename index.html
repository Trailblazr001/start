For Whom

    This course is designed for:

  • AI enthusiasts eager to build confidence when working with LLMs and take their understanding to a deeper level
  • Software developers and engineers interested in customizing LLMs and exploring the creative possibilities of AI
  • Data scientists and researchers looking to unlock new exciting projects and stay ahead of the curve in the AI field
  • Individuals who understand that current investments and interest in AI will be utilized by someone, and want to ensure that someone is them - taking control of their own AI journey and future-proofing their skills

What

    LLM study group is designed to take you on a journey from the basics of Large Language Models to building your own model. Here's a breakdown of what you can expect each week:

  • Week 1: "Pipeline Primer" - Setting Up Your Data Loading Pipeline

    Learn how to implement a data loading pipeline in AIML and review relevant code in PyTorch.

  • Week 2: "Tokenization Tales" - Training a BPE Tokenizer from Scratch

    Watch video tutorials on training a BPE tokenizer and review the chapter on attention mechanisms.

  • Week 3: "Model Mastery" - Implementing the Model Architecture in AIML

    Read the chapters on implementing the model architecture in AIML and start building your LLM.

  • Week 4: "Pretraining Palooza" - Pretraining the LLM

    Watch tutorial videos on pretraining the LLM and read the chapter on pretraining and loading pretrained weights.

  • Week 5: "Fine-Tuning Frenzy" - Fine-Tuning the LLM and Adding Bells and Whistles

    Read the chapters on fine-tuning the LLM and review the code for instruction fine-tuning.

  • Week 6: "Efficient Fine-Tuning" - Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning with LoRA

    Read the provided info on parameter-efficient fine-tuning with LoRA and learn how to optimize your LLM.

  • Week 7: "Coming back to the Roots with C Coding Challenge" - Coding the LLM in C

    Check out coding repositories provided and learn how to code the LLM in C.

  • Final Week: "Build and Share" - Building Something Cool and Sharing it with the World

    Use your newfound skills to build something cool and share it with the world!

How

Our 8-week LLM School is built on top of the following resources:

  • Book"LLMs from Scratch" by Sebastian Raschka
  • Video Materials from Andrej Karpathy
  • Book "Deep Learning" by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville

You can choose from two options to complete the course:

Option 1: Self-Study

  • Goal setting and progress check-in in the middle of the course
  • Final review of the next steps as a touchpoint on the project to build at the end of the course
  • Weekly reminders on the week's content, if you opt in

Option 2: With Community

In addition to all the self-study materials, you'll also get:

  • Small class size for a closer learning experience
  • Online chat with participants for Q&A and aligning on understanding
  • Community study with boosted engagement and motivation
  • Bi-weekly sessions on practical topics such as RAG, Agents, data security, Responsible AI, instruction fine-tuning, and application system design
  • Interactive quizzes to ensure comprehension and assess progress (not just for grades, but to ensure understanding)
